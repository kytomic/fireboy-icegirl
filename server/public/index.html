About

Two players collaborate to escape the temple by solving puzzles, inspired by a childhood browser game “Fireboy and Watergirl 1: Forest Temple”.

Player Control: key up, left, down for jumping, moving left and right.

Win Condition: Both players successfully open the exit door and leave the temple.

Lose Condition: One of the players are burned.

Scores are computed by collected coins and finishing time.
